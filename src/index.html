Welcome to The Red Book of Middle-earth. Discover the legends and lore of J.R.R. Tolkien's world. Dive into the characters, movies and quotes that make Middle-earth so captivating.

Our name comes from a fictional book called "The Red Book of Westmarch" in Tolkien's stories, written by characters like Bilbo and Frodo Baggins. Just like the original Red Book, our site collects and shares the lore and adventures of Middle-earth.
